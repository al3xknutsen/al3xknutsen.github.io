Dream Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20th Feb 2024

Thank you for choosing Sents! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store your information when you use our mobile application. By using Sents, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Data Collection and Usage

a. Personal Information

Sents does not collect or share any personal information directly. We respect your privacy and do not access, store, or share any sensitive information such as your name, address, or contact details.

b. Advertisements

Sents uses Google AdMob to serve ads to users who are not subscribed to the Pro plan. AdMob may collect certain non-personal information for ad targeting purposes. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more information on how they handle data.

c. Income Tracking

To utilize Sents's income tracking feature, users voluntarily provide information about their earnings. This data is stored locally on the user's device and is not collected or shared in any way. Sents does not have access to bank accounts or credit card information.

2. Subscription Information

Sents keeps track of subscriptions purchased through Google Play or Apple AppStore. This information is solely used for managing and providing access to the Pro plan features and is not shared with third parties.

3. Pro Plan Features

a. Camera Functionality

In the Pro plan, Sents offers camera functionality. The user can choose an image to represent a dream that the user wants to save money towards. Images captured are stored locally on the user's device and are not collected or shared by Sents.

b. Access to Device Images

Sents allows users to access images saved on their device. These images are not collected or shared and remain on the user's device exclusively.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any modifications.
